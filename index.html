Case Study: Process Optimization

ABC Manufacturing – Order Processing Optimization

Problem

ABC Manufacturing experienced inefficient order processing due to manual workflows, lack of real-time inventory validation, and poor coordination between departments. This resulted in processing delays, frequent errors, and low customer satisfaction.

Objective

To redesign the order processing system using BPM principles, improving process efficiency, reducing errors, and enabling better scalability across departments.

Approach

  • Analyzed the existing workflow to identify bottlenecks and inefficiencies
  • Designed an advanced BPMN model with decision gateways for key processes
  • Integrated loops for inventory rechecks and payment retries
  • Implemented exception handling for cancellations, returns, and failed payments
  • Structured interactions across Sales, Warehouse, and Finance departments

Impact

  • Improved process visibility and workflow structure
  • Reduced operational errors through validation and automation logic
  • Enhanced coordination between departments
  • Created a scalable and maintainable process design
